Film Techniques: Pyscho

Psycho

By Ayinde Castro

1st Picture (Long Shot):

During the scene where Mario Crane is trying to purchase a car and the policeman watches her from across the street, Alfred Hitchcock uses a LONG SHOT. By using a long shot, Alfred Hitchcock is able to let the viewer know that the police man is still following Marion and trying to figure out what mischief she is up to.

2nd Picture (Over The Back):

When Marion Crane is being questioned by the police officer, Hitcock uses an OVER THE BACK shot. By using this shot, he is able to distinguish a relationship betweent he two.
